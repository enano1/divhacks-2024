What is a Sustainability-Linked Loan?

1. Terms are tied to sustainability performance
2. Based on key performance indicators (KPIs)
3. Corporate responsibility and ESG criteria are growing concerns
4. Reputation boost for businesses aligning with ESG trends
5. Improved financing for sustainable borrowers

Problem

SLLs are usually for large companies where terms are custom-created
KPIs can be difficult to keep track of

Solution

1. B2B SaaS Solution allowing small businesses to apply directly for SLLs
2. Automatically comes up with terms
3. Keeps track of SDG performance and standing
