What is a Sustainability-Linked Loan?

Terms are tied to sustainability performance
Based on key performance indicators (KPIs)
Corporate responsibility and ESG criterias are growing concerns
Reputation boost for businesses aligning with ESG trends
Improved financing for sustainable borrowers

Problem

SLLs are usually for large companies where terms are custom-created
KPIs can be difficult to keep track of

Solution

B2B SaaS Solution allowing small businesses to apply directly for SLLs
Automatically comes up with terms
Keeps track of SDG performance and standing
